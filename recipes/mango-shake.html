Mango Shake Recipe

Description

Mango shake is a refreshing and delicious beverage, perfect for hot summer days. Made with ripe, juicy mangoes blended with milk, sugar, and a hint of cardamom, itâ€™s a creamy and naturally sweet drink loved by all age groups. Its tropical flavor and smooth texture make it a favorite treat. Packed with nutrients like vitamins A and C, this simple yet delightful drink is not only a quick thirst-quencher but also a healthy option. You can customize it with ice cream, nuts, or a splash of vanilla for an added twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe mangoes (peeled and diced)
  • 2 cups chilled milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Chopped nuts or whipped cream for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Mangoes: Peel and dice the ripe mangoes. Remove the seeds and cut the flesh into small cubes.
  2. Blend the Ingredients: In a blender, combine the mango cubes, chilled milk, sugar, and cardamom powder (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Adjust Consistency: If the shake is too thick, add a little more milk or a few ice cubes and blend again to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Serve: Pour the mango shake into glasses. Garnish with chopped nuts, a dollop of whipped cream, or a slice of mango if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
